The Problem

- Kids with autism use phrases instead of sentences to express their thoughts
- Phrases sound meaningless, but can convey emotions ranging from joy to sadness
- Parents new to autism might not know what these phrases mean, and they are often unable to devote enough time to find out

lightbulb_outline

The solution

- Journal which compiles all aspects of the situation surrounding the child's verbalization of their phrase

- Audio can capture all instances child says the word/phrase
- Upload the audio onto website so tone processing API can process the tone of the speech
- Space for parent to note what they have done
- All "evidence" is compiled onto another web page for the parent to refer to and draw conclusions

OUR PURPOSE

Our goal is to enable parents to bond with their children, without requiring the use of words.
